Frequently Asked Questions

Is this an official app?

No. This is an independent community tool created to help people locate and connect with local chapters.

How do I find chapters near me?

Use the Chapters tab to browse all chapters, or use the search feature to filter by your state or city.

Can I create a new chapter?

The app allows you to view and submit chapter information. Please verify with official sources for chapter registration requirements.

How do I update my profile?

Go to the Profile tab and tap the Edit button to update your information.
