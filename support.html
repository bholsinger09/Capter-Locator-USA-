‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find chapters near me?
Navigate to the Chapters tab, use the search bar to find chapters by name or location, or filter by your state using the state picker.
How do I join a chapter?
Go to your Profile tab, tap "Edit", select your state and choose a chapter from the available options. You can also view chapter details and contact information from the Chapters tab.
Can I create a new chapter?
Yes! Tap the "+" button in the Chapters tab to submit information for a new chapter. Please provide accurate contact and meeting information.
How do I update my profile?
Go to the Profile tab and tap the "Edit" button in the top right corner. You can update your personal information, state, and university.
Is this the official TPUSA app?
No, this is an independent community tool. For official Turning Point USA resources, visit www.tpusa.com
How is my data stored?
All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect or transmit your personal information to any external servers.
I found a bug. How do I report it?
Please email us at bholsinger09@gmail.com with details about the issue, including your device model and iOS version.
